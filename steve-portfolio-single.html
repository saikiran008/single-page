FAQ

Common Questions

More questions? WhatsApp me — I reply within a few hours.


Ask Me →
Do I need to provide anything to get started?
+

Just your business name, what your business does, contact details, and any images or logo you have. I'll handle everything else — design, structure, content layout, and going live.

How long does a website take?
+

Most standard business websites are ready within 48–72 hours. E-commerce stores take 5–7 days. I always give you a clear timeline before starting.

What about hosting and domain — who pays for that?
+

Hosting and domain are separate from my development charge. I'll help you buy them at the best price (usually ₹800–₹2,000/year) and set everything up. Or if you have hosting, I'll work with that.

Can I update the website myself after it's done?
+

Yes — I build sites with a simple admin panel so you can update text, images, and content yourself without any coding knowledge. I'll give you a walkthrough after delivery.

Do you work with clients outside Rajahmundry?
+

Absolutely. I work with clients all across India via WhatsApp, video calls, and email. Distance is no barrier — most of the work is done online.

What payment methods do you accept?
+

I accept UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), bank transfer, and cash for local clients. 50% advance before starting, 50% after delivery.

Will my website show up on Google?
+

All websites include basic on-page SEO — proper titles, meta descriptions, fast loading, and mobile-friendliness. For advanced SEO, I offer a separate optimisation service.
